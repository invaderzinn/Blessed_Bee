Tarot Explained

There are a lot of myths and misconceptions surrounding Tarot. For example, plenty of people think drawing the Death card means either you or a loved one will soon die. This is far from the truth! Here, we like to help our customers learn more about Tarot to dispell those myths and help them become comfortable with their reading.
Some people are afraid or even skeptical of their first reading, for the afforementioned reasons and others. This is a safe space where we guide our clients to find CLARITY in their lives. We just have the tools to help draw them out so you can see your Truth more clearly.
